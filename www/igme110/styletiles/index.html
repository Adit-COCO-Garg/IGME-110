I want my course website to feel up-to-date and modern, since I'm teaching students modern web technologies. It needs to use a typeface that is easy to read on both low and high resolution monitors, and when projected. Colors should be for accent and emphasis only, because the emphasis should be on the text content, so I stuck with black text on a white background, and a magenta highlight used only for links.
